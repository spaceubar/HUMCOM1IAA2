HOW TO COOK?

Sarciadong Galunggong

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/2 kilo Galunggong (cleaned)
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • Oil for fying
    Sarciado sauce

  • 1 piece onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 6 pieces tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 piece egg
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Season the fish with salt. Rub it well. Marinate at least 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan, and fry the fish until golden brown.
  3. After frying, set aside.
  4. For the Sarciado sauce. Heat a small amount of oil in a frying pan then saut√© onions, garlic, and tomatoes until crushed. Press to crush the tomatoes. Add water and let it boil.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add the beaten egg to the crushed tomato sauce. Start stirring the mixture until the egg curdles or coagulates.
  6. Add fried fish and simmer for about a minute.
  7. Serve it while it is still hot. Enjoy!
